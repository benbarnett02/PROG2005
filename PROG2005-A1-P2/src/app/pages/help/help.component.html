Help

For help with this application, please see the below list of topics.

Adding a New Client

To add a new client, click the "Add Client" button on the Clients page. Enter the client's details in the form and click "Submit". The new client will be added to the list of clients.

Viewing and Editing Clients

To view or edit an existing client, click on the View/Edit button next to the client's name in the list of clients on the Clients page. You can then view or edit the client's details in the form that appears.

Deleting a Client

To delete a client, click on the Delete button next to the client's name in the list of clients on the Clients page. The client will be removed from the list of clients.

Filtering Clients

You can filter the list of clients by entering a search term in the search bar at the top of the Clients page. The list of clients will be filtered to show only those clients whose name or email address matches the search term.

You may also see a list of VIP clients by clicking on the VIP Clients list link from the home page.
